What is it like to live in Valencia? Would you recommend it? What is the city like?

Although it's a big city, everything is well connected and it is easy to get around. Would definitely recommend it, you have the city break on one side and then ten minutes away you have the beach break!

What is the student lifestyle like in Valencia?

It can be quite difficult to make friends with local Spaniards as they tend to go home at the weekends and they keep to themselves. But there is a great Erasmus community that makes the student lifestyle great!

How much does it cost to live in Valencia?

To go for drinks out it is quite expensive. But to buy food and drinks to have in the flat/house is quite cheap.
